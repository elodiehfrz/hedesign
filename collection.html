Collection Dans Ma Poche

Collection Dans Ma Poche, made with the help of influencers from around the world, is a travel book series created to help you organize a memorable trip. Between small restaurants, international youth hostels, tourist sites, parks and gardens, we take you through irresistible places in the biggest cities of the world. The first book of this collection is dedicated to New York City.
